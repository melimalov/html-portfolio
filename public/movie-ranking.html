The Best Animated Movies According to Meli

My top 3 animated movies of all time

Spirited Away

One of the best animated movies I have ever watched, the characters and animations inspired me so much

Shrek

I just believe this is one of those sophisticated films that make you laugh and will go down in history as one of the best

The Nightmare Before Christmas

This is just that perfect movie directed by Tim Burton that hits me in the feels with the awesome musical tones and a balance between Halloween-y and Christmas-sy vibes
